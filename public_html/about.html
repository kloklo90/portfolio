KHADY SECK

I am a computer engineer passionate of Front End Development. I like clean and sharp designs whether my canvas is a website, an android app or a plain old Desktop application (Eclipse RCP GUIs). I strongly believe that less is more in the UI/UX world; well that and going over several iterations to make sure my design is intuitive and enjoyable for the targeted users. But above all I like to think differently and challenge norms to propose new valuable ideas and insights.

I am built to handle high pressure environments. I kid you not, I actually love working in a startup-like environment where no one gives up and the road to success is paved with continuous learning and growth. Although I am a very “independent and autonomous worker” ditto of one of my previous employer, I find that my ideas and work ethic thrive in team environments.

Besides work stuff, I am a very social developer. So you can catch me at coding meetups like Montreal Ruby or debating with poli sci geniuses every day of the week… after work hours of course ;). No I am not a workaholic; it’s all part of the fun.
